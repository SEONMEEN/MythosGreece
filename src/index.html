The Olympian Gods

Zeus

Domain

King of the Gods, God of the Sky & Thunder

Symbol

Thunderbolt, Eagle

Story

Defeated the Titans and ruled Mount Olympus. Known for his many affairs and offspring.

Hera

Domain

Goddess of Marriage and Family

Symbol

Peacock, Diadem

Story

Wife of Zeus, often portrayed as jealous and vengeful, but deeply protective of marriage.

Poseidon

Domain

God of the Sea

Symbol

Trident, Horse

Story

Creator of horses and earthquakes. Known for his moody and tempestuous nature.

Famous Myths & Legends

1. The Trojan War

The legendary war fought over Helen of Troy. Key figures include: Achilles, Hector, Paris, and Odysseus.

2. Medusa & Perseus

Perseus, son of Zeus, defeats the snake-haired Gorgon and uses her head as a weapon.

3. Orpheus and Eurydice

A tragic love story. Orpheus travels to the Underworld to bring back his beloved, only to lose her again.
